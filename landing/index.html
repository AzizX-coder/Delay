📖 Full Documentation

Delay v3.0 introduces a seamless and powerful workflow for the modern professional. Here is everything you need to know about the tools included.

1. AI Copilot (Copilot)

Delay features a powerful AI assistant that can be configured to use Local Models (Ollama) for complete privacy, or Cloud Models (OpenRouter, Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic) for maximum intelligence. During onboarding, you can easily select your preferred provider or disable the AI features entirely.

  • Global command palette Cmd/Ctrl + K for instant AI access.
  • Chat context includes recent notes and tasks.

2. Capture & Vault

We replaced complex disk flows with two powerful new concepts:

  • Capture (formerly Saved): A global inbox. Hit Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + S anywhere to capture a quick thought, link, or image. Like a personal Telegram "Saved Messages" but better.
  • Vault (formerly Bucket): Secure, local file storage. Upload files and they remain strictly on your device, protected by the App Lock PIN if configured.

3. Kanban & Tasks

A full Trello-style Kanban board is built right in. With the new v3 update, you can drag and drop cards, open a Detail Drawer to set due dates, priorities, and write rich descriptions, and view a complete activity log for every card.

4. Gamification

Productivity is now a game. Earn XP by completing tasks, finishing Pomodoro focus sessions, and writing notes. Level up and see your stats grow on the Status Dashboard.
