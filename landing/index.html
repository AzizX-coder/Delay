📖 Full Documentation

Everything you need to know about setting up and using Delay effectively.

Installation

Delay is available on three platforms:

  • Windows — Download the .exe installer from GitHub Releases
  • Android / Tablet — Download the .apk from the same Releases page. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device settings.
  • Web — Visit this page and the app runs directly in your browser (limited features).

Getting Started

On first launch, Delay will guide you through a quick onboarding:

  • Choose your visual theme (Aura Light, Obsidian, Forest, Mocha, Ocean, Rosé)
  • Select your language preference
  • Customize which modules to enable from the sidebar

AI Configuration

AI features are entirely optional. By default, AI is enabled but you must configure a provider. If you don't want AI at all, simply toggle it off in Settings → AI Intelligence.

Bucket — Your Private Vault

The Bucket works like Telegram's "Saved Messages". Send yourself text notes, links, and files. Everything is stored locally in your device's protected app storage. You can:

  • Pin important messages to the top
  • Search across all saved items
  • Upload and download files (images, PDFs, etc.)
  • Access your saved items instantly — even offline

App Security

Set a 4-digit PIN in Settings → App Security to lock the entire app behind a PIN screen on every launch. This protects your notes, bucket data, conversations, and all private content from unauthorized physical access.

Timer — Focus Mode

The Pomodoro Timer features three modes: Focus, Short Break, and Long Break. When the timer is running, the device's screen will stay awake (via the Wake Lock API) so your session never gets interrupted by auto-sleep.

Themes

Delay ships with 7 hand-crafted themes. Each theme adjusts the entire UI palette including backgrounds, borders, accents, and text colors:

  • Aura Light — Clean white interface
  • Obsidian — Deep dark mode
  • Forest — Earthy greens
  • Mocha — Warm coffee tones
  • Ocean — Cool blues
  • Rosé — Soft pinks
  • System — Automatically follows your OS preference

Privacy & Security

Delay is designed with a local-first architecture. Here's what that means:

  • All data (notes, tasks, events, files) is stored exclusively on your device using IndexedDB and localStorage
  • No data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly connect to an AI provider
  • AI is optional — you can disable it entirely and the app works fully offline
  • The Bucket stores files inside the app's protected sandbox (on Android, inaccessible to other apps)
  • The PIN lock adds a physical access barrier for shared devices
