Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Four focused modules, one beautifully quiet app.

Rich Notes

A clean editor with formatting, checklists, highlights and color-coded notes. No red underlines — just write.

Smart Tasks

Priorities, due dates and smart views for Today, Upcoming and Completed. Stay focused without the noise.

Calendar

Plan your days in a quiet month view. Colored events, quick scheduling, your whole month at a glance.

Local AI

Chat with your local Ollama models. Streaming responses, conversation history — nothing leaves your machine.

Private by design.

Delay has no servers. Your notes, tasks, events and AI chats live in a local IndexedDB database on your own device.
